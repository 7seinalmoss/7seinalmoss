Delicious Protein Pancakes

Healthy, tasty, and packed with protein!

Ingredients

  • 1 scoop protein powder
  • 1/2 cup oats
  • 1/2 cup Greek yogurt
  • 2 egg whites
  • 1/4 tsp baking powder
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • Optional: berries, honey, or syrup

Instructions

  1. Blend oats into a fine powder (optional).
  2. Mix all ingredients in a bowl until smooth.
  3. Heat a non-stick pan over medium heat and lightly grease it.
  4. Pour batter to form small pancakes and cook for 2-3 minutes per side.
  5. Serve warm with your favorite toppings.

Tips

For extra fluffiness, add a pinch of baking soda and a splash of lemon juice!
